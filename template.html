unfold_more

An entrepreneur at heart, I have been a professor, a writer, and many times a group leader.

As an entrepreneur, I founded TeraDeep and FWDNXT. FWDNXT was acquired by Micron Inc. in 2019 for our pioneering work on deep neural network processors.

As a writer, I have co-authored 3 books and more than 100 peer-reviewed papers. I follow the development of the tech world and focus on the ripples rather than the bubbles. I focus on new applications of artificial intelligence that can forward humanity.

As a university professor at Yale and Purdue I have worked on Intelligent Vision Systems, Biomedical Circuits and Systems, and Silicon-on-Sapphire Circuits and Systems. My PhD from Johns Hopkins University was on analog and mixed-signal microchip design for neuromorphic applications. I was one of the pioneers in spike-based imaging.

My recent work is focused on the creation of an artificial scientist using multi-modal large world models.
